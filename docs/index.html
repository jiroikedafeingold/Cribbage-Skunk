Cribbage Skunk

Two-player cribbage scoring for the phone laid between you.

Built for the table

Cribbage Skunk lives between two players. Each side of the screen belongs to one of you — controls rotated, score on the board, peg climbing toward 121.

🎯

Drag-to-add scoring

Drag the slider on your side. Release. The points commit, the slider snaps back, the peg climbs.

⚡

Quick +1 for pegging

Tap the +1 chip during pegging instead of fiddling with the slider for one point at a time.

↩️

Per-player undo

Miscount? Tap undo to pop the last add. Each player has their own history.

🦨

Skunk & double skunk

The 60 and 90 lines are marked on the board. The celebration scales with the win — animated skunks, confetti, and escalating haptics.

👑

Faces both players

The board symbols and the winner card periodically rotate so both players get a clear, upright view.

🎨

Names & colors

Pick a name and one of eight color themes per player. Customization persists across launches.

💾

Never lose a game

Scores survive force-quits and restarts. Pick up an in-progress game days later if you have to.

🚫

No ads, no tracking

No accounts, no analytics, no advertising. Nothing leaves your phone.

How it works

No setup. No login. Just open the app and play.

  1. Set the table

    Lay the phone flat between you and your opponent. Each player's panel faces the side they're sitting on.

  2. Deal a hand

    Play cribbage as you always do — pegging, the show, his nibs, all of it. Bring your own deck.

  3. Score on your side

    When you count up your hand, drag the slider on your side to the total and release. Your peg climbs.

  4. First to 121 wins

    Cross the line and the celebration fires. If your opponent didn't make it past 90, you skunked them. Past 60? Double skunk.

Privacy Policy

Last updated · May 27, 2026

The short version

Cribbage Skunk does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

What the app stores on your device

So your game survives closing the app, Cribbage Skunk saves the following locally using iOS's standard preferences storage (UserDefaults):

  • Each player's current score
  • The current winner and skunk status (if a game has finished)
  • The names you set for each player
  • The color theme you chose for each player

This data never leaves your iPhone. It is not transmitted to us, to Apple beyond what iOS does for normal app function, or to any third party.

What the app does not collect

  • No name, email address, or account information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers used for tracking
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting beyond what the App Store itself may provide to Apple
  • No advertising identifiers

Third-party services

Cribbage Skunk does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or back-end servers. It runs entirely on your device.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Deleting your data

To remove all stored data — scores, names, color choices — uninstall the app from your device. Deleting the app removes all local storage with it. You can also use the "Reset Scores" button in the in-app settings sheet to zero out an in-progress game without losing your player names or colors.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and the new version will appear on this page.
